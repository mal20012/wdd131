Site Name

Dream Destinations — a digital bucket list map website where users can visually track and showcase the places they dream of visiting or have already explored. The name captures a sense of wanderlust and personal adventure, perfect for a travel-themed website.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Dream Destinations is to provide users with an interactive, visually appealing way to map out their travel goals and reflect on past adventures. It will feature a bucket list-style map, a gallery for memories, and background information about the user’s travel inspiration.

Scenarios

  • "Where have I traveled to already, and where do I want to go next?"
  • "Can I see a visual map of someone’s dream travel list and their favorite memories?"

Color Schema

  • #ccd5ae – Light Sage (header/footer background)
  • #e9edc9 – Soft Olive (navigation background)
  • #fefae0 – Cream (body background)
  • #faedcd – Peach Beige (main section background)
  • #d4a373 – Warm Brown (headings and hover effects)

Typography

  • Playfair Display – Used for headings to give an elegant, classic touch
  • Raleway – Used for body text and navigation for modern readability
