🌍 Bucket List Map

Your personal journey across the world

1. Site Name

Name: Bucket List Map

This name reflects the purpose of the site — to visually display dream destinations on a map. It's memorable, descriptive, and fits the adventurous theme.

2. Site Purpose

This website helps users track their dream travel destinations and document their travel memories on an interactive map. It serves as both a personal inspiration board and a digital travel journal.

3. Scenarios

  • "Where have I traveled so far, and what’s next on my list?"
  • "Can I see photos and memories from the places I’ve already visited?"

4. Color Schema

  • #ccd5ae – Background color (light sage green)
  • #e9edc9 – Section backgrounds and soft accents
  • #fefae0 – Primary content background
  • #faedcd – Navigation bar and hover effects
  • #d4a373 – Headings, buttons, and key highlights

5. Typography

  • Playfair Display – Used for all major headings and titles
  • Roboto – Used for body text and navigation links
